Collaboration in Line

Simulation

This is a collaborative simulation of a physical light installation. Together, participants mount LED light strips and diffusion frames onto a shared metal grid.

LED Strip Place one 20 ft strip of light by clicking mounting points on the grid. The strip hangs with gravity between points. Click Place Line to commit. Each strip has a maximum length of 20 feet — the remaining footage is shown as you place. When mounting points turn red, the line has reached its limit. Shorten the line by dragging points closer together to unlock editing.
Frames Place one small (8"×8") and one large (2'×2') diffusion frame. Light passing behind a frame glows softly through the fabric.
Editing Select the corresponding tool, then click an element to edit it. Drag mounting points to reshape lines. You may edit others' work, but their start and end points are locked.
View & Export Use View mode to pan and inspect the installation. Scroll to zoom in. Download a snapshot with the PDF button. The timeline at the bottom shows each contributor's session.
Leaderboard Click Rank to rate each line in the composition on a 5-star scale. Half-star ratings are supported. Once you have rated every line, the community leaderboard is revealed. New lines must be rated before viewing updated rankings. Click any entry on the leaderboard to highlight that line on the canvas.

If you experience any issues with placement or performance, refresh the page and log back in with the same name and email. Your work is saved automatically.
